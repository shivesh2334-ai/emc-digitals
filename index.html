// About Us

Clinicians Who
Code and Create

Founded by Dr. Shivesh Kumar — a cardiologist with 14+ years of clinical experience and academic credentials from IIM Calcutta, IISc Bangalore, and Annamalai University — EMC Digitals was born at the intersection of medicine and modern AI.

We build tools for doctors, by doctors. Our workflow takes clinical insights directly to functional MVPs using Streamlit, Next.js, React, and frontier AI APIs. Based in Dwarka, Delhi, our products serve learners and practitioners across India.

Our Journey

From the first prototype to a growing portfolio — built across clinical shifts, on an iPad, with passion.

Early 2025

The Beginning

First marketing strategy app launched on Streamlit. EasyMyCare Clinic prescription SaaS enters development.

Mid 2025

Medical Education Tools

ABG interpreter, Hypertension tool, and Altitude sickness apps deployed. Safe2Peak goes live.

Late 2025

AI-Powered Stack

ECG, CXR, and PFT learning apps launch. EasyMyCare Clinic goes to production. AMR research data collection deployed.

January 2026

Medical Gamification + Certs

Hospital Board Game, Medical Certificate Generator, and AUD Rehab system ship. Doctor Marketing Platform launched.

February 2026

Cardiology & Pulmonology Suite

Tilt Test, COPD treatment, CXR v2, Smoking Cessation, and Blood Analysis Tool all deployed. MRCP Roadmap goes live.

March 2026+

Open for Collaboration

Volume Overload Guide and Vitamin Guide shipped. Portfolio open for partnerships, investment, and co-development.
